WHAT IS CUTIFY

cutiFY is an innovative music platform launched on October 11, 2024, that celebrates Original Pilipino Music (OPM). Designed to cater to music lovers who appreciate local talent, cutiFY offers an extensive collection of tracks from both up-and-coming and established OPM artists. Beyond just streaming, the platform allows users to dive deeper into the stories behind their favorite artists, providing a unique, immersive experience for anyone passionate about Filipino music culture.

FOUNDER OF CUTIFY

Founded by Sean Ivan Fabia(left) and Paolo Leandro Pinca(right), both 19 years old, cutiFY is the product of their shared passion for OPM and their desire to create a space where Filipino artists can shine. Their vision is to provide a platform where OPM artists are not only heard but also understood on a more personal level. With their fresh perspective and commitment to uplifting local talent, they are on a mission to make cutiFY a go-to destination for OPM enthusiasts worldwide. Click here to get to know them more.

CUTIFY X INGAY-TUGTOG

This platform was made possible through a key collaboration with Adielyn Quitorio(right) and Bhellejhun Caparal(left) of Ingay-Tugtog, a company dedicated to promoting local sound.

Together, they have combined their expertise in music, technology, and business to ensure that cutiFY offers a seamless experience for both artists and listeners.
